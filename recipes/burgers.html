Burger Recipe

Description

This is a recipe for juicy cheese burgers. Nothing beats a burger on the weekends, and these burgers are guranteed to be a hit.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • American cheese
  • Mayonaise
  • Ketchup
  • Buns
  • lettuce
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Form ground beef into patties and cook in pan or on grill until medium
  2. Lightly toast buns
  3. Add lettuce and tomato onto the bottom bun
  4. Place cooked patty on top followed by the American cheese
  5. Add desired amount of Ketchup and Mayo
  6. Enjoy your burger!
